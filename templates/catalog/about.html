About CourseCompass

CourseCompass helps learners figure out exactly which skills they're missing for their dream career, and recommends the best courses to close that gap.

Tell us your career goal and current skills, and we'll compare them against what the role actually requires — then search our course catalog to find the courses that cover what you're missing.

Built as a student project covering Python fundamentals, OOP, MongoDB, and Django.
